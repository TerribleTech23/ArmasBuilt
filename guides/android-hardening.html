Android De-Googling

Strategic steps for moving your digital life away from big-tech tracking and toward localized privacy.

Step 01

Private DNS

The first step is moving your DNS traffic away from your ISP. Use NextDNS or AdGuard DNS to block trackers at the system level.

Step 02

Localized Input

Standard keyboards send your keystrokes to the cloud for "prediction." Use FUTO Keyboard for localized voice-to-text that never leaves your device.
