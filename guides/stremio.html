Stremio + Real-Debrid

The core setup for a high-speed, buffer-free media experience. Eliminate ISP throttling and peer-dependency.

Pro Tip: Using a Debrid service encrypted via HTTPS means you don't technically need a VPN for privacy while streaming, though it's always recommended for overall security.
Step 01

Acquire Real-Debrid

Real-Debrid acts as your personal "downloader" in the cloud. It fetches torrents on high-speed servers and serves them to you as a direct download.

Get Real-Debrid
Step 02

Configure Torrentio

Torrentio is the bridge. You must configure it in a web browser before it appears in your app.

  • Visit the Torrentio configuration page.
  • Select Real-Debrid as the provider.
  • Paste your API Token into the field.
Configure Torrentio
Step 03

Optimize Your Device

On your S25 Ultra or Onn 4K, ensure these settings are enabled in Stremio:

  • Hardware Acceleration: ON
  • Frame Rate Matching: Enabled
